Lucy

Inspiration

Hi, I am Tanvi's cute yet very dignified cat. The team loves animals and felt image recognition for our beloved pets is something we should explore more, with many lost animals this would be a great addition to finding our lost friends

To add to her analytic insights and improve her techinical ability in coding, she began this course at Trilogy. Fun Fact: "My favourite coding language is python"
